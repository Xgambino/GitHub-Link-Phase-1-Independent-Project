THE PROCESS

Working with us is extremely easy! The process is simple and straightforward as follows:

1. Pre-Shoot Consultation:

Every shoot is different, customized to our clients’ preferences, so prior consultation helps be sure we understand what you’re looking for and walk you through the process. We often speak with the clients by phone and/or meet in person ahead of time to review examples of what they’re looking for. Using this form, you can send us sample photos and/or links you find in our portfolio or elsewhere that you might like to recreate during our photo shoot. That’s especially helpful to help us prepare for your shoot.

2. Pick a Date for the Photo Shoot:

It’s important to give yourself plenty of time in advance of your deadline. We recommend an absolute minimum of one week. Add at least an additional two weeks if you want a photo album, framed print or other printed product. (We can provide rush service for an additional fee of KES2000)

3. The “Paperwork”:

Once you select a date/time for the shoot, you're required to pay at least 50% of the price of the bouquet of your interest. Once received, we’ll book the studio time and confirm your shoot. (We can’t confirm your shoot until we receive your deposit.)

4. The Shoot!:

The shoot will be carried out in the venue of your convenience. We arrive at least an hour prior to set up the venue. If you’ve requested a makeup artist, s/he will meet you at the studio at the beginning of your session.
