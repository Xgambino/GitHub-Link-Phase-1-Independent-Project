THE PROCESS

Working with us is extremely easy! The process is simple and
straightforward as follows:

1. Pre-Shoot Consultation:

Every shoot is different, customized to our clients’ preferences, so
prior consultation helps be sure we understand what you’re looking for
and walk you through the process.
We often speak with the clients by
phone and/or meet in person ahead of time to review examples of what
they’re looking for.
Using this form, you can send us sample photos
and/or links you find in our portfolio or elsewhere that you might
like to recreate during our photo shoot.
That’s especially helpful to help us prepare for your shoot.

2. Pick a Date for the Photo Shoot:

It’s important to give yourself plenty of time in advance of your
deadline. We recommend an absolute minimum of one week. Add at least
an additional two weeks if you want a photo album, framed print or
other printed product. (We can provide rush service for an additional
fee of KES2000)

3. The “Paperwork”:

Once you select a date/time for the shoot, you're required to pay at
least 50% of the price of the bouquet of your interest. Once received,
we’ll book the studio time and confirm your shoot. (We can’t confirm
your shoot until we receive your deposit.)

4. The Shoot!:

The shoot will be carried out in the venue of your convenience. We
will provide you with a list of possible shooting locations. You can
arrive at least an hour prior to set up the venue. If you’ve requested
a makeup artist, s/he will meet you at the studio at the beginning of
your session.

Location

Imagine capturing the majestic hues of a sunset casting a warm glow over rugged mountain peaks,
or the tranquil beauty of a secluded beach at dawn,
kissed by the first light of day.
Picture yourself amidst the bustling energy of a vibrant city,
where towering skyscrapers and bustling streets provide endless opportunities for urban exploration and street photography.
Venture into the heart of nature, where lush forests, cascading waterfalls, and winding rivers beckon with their raw and untamed beauty.
From historic landmarks steeped in culture and heritage to hidden gems off the beaten path, the world is a canvas waiting to be explored through the lens of your camera. Whether you seek the serenity of untouched landscapes
or the dynamic energy of bustling city streets, there's a photography location out there to ignite your imagination and inspire your next masterpiece.
